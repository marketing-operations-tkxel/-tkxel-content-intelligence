## 4. Lead / pipeline targets (two levers)

`Δ Leads = (Δ Sessions × CR) + (Sessions × Δ CR)`

- From traffic: +1,080 organic × 0.78% = **+8.4 leads/mo**
- From CRO: +0.2pp CR on existing organic = **+10.8 leads/mo** (faster — multiplies across all traffic)
- **Q3 lead commitment:** +12–18 genuine leads/mo blended; **1+ new Qualified MQL/mo** from content-sourced pipeline.

## 6. The lag, stated up front

New-content traffic lands **~1 quarter out** (3–6 month maturation). Q3's 15 new articles mostly pay off in **Q4**. Optimizations carry the current quarter. **Do not expect "we published all month, where's the traffic?" math in Week 6** — that's not how organic compounds.
